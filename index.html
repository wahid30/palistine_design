Clean Water

"Access to clean water is a fundamental human right that should be guaranteed for all people, including those in Palestine. Every individual deserves access to safe and clean drinking water to live a healthy and dignified life. It's imperative for governments, organizations, and individuals to come together to address the water crisis in Palestine and work towards sustainable solutions. Let's advocate, support, and take action to ensure that clean water becomes a reality for every Palestinian community."

Educations

"Education is the key to empowerment and progress for Palestine. By investing in education, we invest in the future of our nation. Let us prioritize building schools, providing quality education, and fostering an environment where every Palestinian child has the opportunity to learn, grow, and achieve their full potential. Education is not just a right; it is a pathway to a brighter, more prosperous future for all Palestinians. Together, let's build a stronger, more educated Palestine."

Healthy Food

"Embrace the richness of Palestine's culinary heritage by nourishing your body with wholesome, locally sourced ingredients. Let every meal be a celebration of health, flavor, and tradition. Your generosity will help rebuild lives and communities, bringing comfort and relief to those who need it most. Palestinian Refugee Camp Attacked by ISIS

A few miles outside of Damascus, Syria,
the Yarmouk refugee camp for Palestinians has come
under fire from the self-proclaimed
Islamic State. Over the weekend, about 2,000 people managed
to flee after two exits were opened, but
Palestinian officials report that ISIS
has control of 60 percent of the camp.
As many as 18,000 civilians remain trapped.
Weighing in on the situation is Christopher
Gunness, a spokesman for the United Nations
Relief and Works Agency for Palestine Refugees.
